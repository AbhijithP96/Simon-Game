How To Play

In this interactive game, the player engages by triggering a tile to blink, accompanied by an associated sound when they press a key on their keyboard.

Subsequently, the player is tasked with the challenge of selecting the correct tile in response.

As the game progresses, a new tile is introduced into the sequence, requiring the player to memorize and replicate the entire sequence accurately.

To successfully navigate the game, the player must recall and select the tiles from the initial sequence in the correct order.

This game not only tests memory and reaction time but also offers an engaging and immersive experience for players looking for a mental challenge and entertainment.
